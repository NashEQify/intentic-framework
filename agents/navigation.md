# Navigation: agents/

## What lives here?

**Orchestrator-neutral persona definitions** (source of truth).
Adapters under `orchestrators/<name>/` and `.claude/agents/`
reference these files — they don't duplicate, they reference.

**Persona clusters:**
- **Buddy** (`buddy/`) — the orchestrator itself (soul,
  operational, boot, permissions, wakeup). Its own navigation
  in `buddy/navigation.md`.
- **Spec Board** (board-chief, board-adversary[-2],
  board-implementer, board-impact, board-consumer) —
  multi-perspective spec review.
- **UX Board** (board-ux-heuristic, board-ux-ia,
  board-ux-interaction) — UX review as `spec_board mode=ux`.
- **Code Review Board** (code-chief, code-review, code-adversary,
  code-security, code-data, code-reliability, code-domain-logic,
  code-api-contract, code-ai-llm, code-spec-fit, code-spec-drift,
  code-docs-consumer) — diff review L1 / L2.
- **Standalone** (main-code-agent, council-member,
  solution-expert, security, tester, test-skeleton-writer,
  plan-adversary).

**Persona classification:** ~25 DISTINCT, multi-axis hybrid
(code-quality + code-architecture + code-performance absorbed
into the multi-axis `code-review`). plan-adversary as a
HYBRID stub (persona definition in
`_protocols/plan-review.md`).

**`_protocols/`:** persona-level protocols (reviewer base,
reasoning trace, first principles, board-specific protocols).
Its own navigation in `_protocols/navigation.md`.
