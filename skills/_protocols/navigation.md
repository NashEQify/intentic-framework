# Navigation: _protocols/

## What lives here?

**Skill-level protocols** — cross-cutting required mechanics loaded by
multiple skills, never called directly. Cross-loading pattern: a skill
references the protocol by path; the loader inlines it into the prompt
assembly step when needed.

Separation: skill = capability (what happens), protocol = mechanic
(how it happens with discipline). When a mechanic appears in more
than one skill in identical form, it belongs here — not duplicated
inline.

Boundary against `agents/_protocols/`: those are persona-level
protocols (reviewer base, reasoning trace, first principles). The
ones here are skill-level.
